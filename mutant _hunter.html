About Mutant Hunter

Mutant Hunter is an immersive action game where you embark on dangerous quests to hunt down mutated creatures. Set in a post-apocalyptic world, you must survive the onslaught of monstrous enemies while upgrading your arsenal to become the ultimate hunter.




Faction 1: The Iron Syndicate

The Iron Syndicate is a ruthless industrial cartel that emerged from the remnants of pre-apocalypse megacorporations...

Rustknights

Backstory: Rustknights are the heavily armored enforcers of the Syndicate...

Advantages:

  • Extremely durable due to their heavy armor.
  • Armed with improvised but devastating weapons like buzzsaw blades and flamethrowers.

Disadvantages:

  • Slow and cumbersome, making them easy to outmaneuver.
  • Vulnerable to ranged attacks due to poor mobility.

Scrapdogs

Backstory: These scavengers are the Syndicate's foot soldiers...

Advantages:

  • Quick and resourceful, able to repurpose the environment into traps or tools.
  • Attack in swarms, overwhelming their enemies.

Disadvantages:

  • Poorly equipped compared to other factions.
  • Low morale; prone to fleeing when outmatched.

Smog Priests

Backstory: Smog Priests are twisted scientists who worship industrial pollution...

Advantages:

  • Their toxic clouds can disrupt visibility and corrode armor.
  • Act as support units, enhancing allies with stimulants or weakening enemies with poisons.

Disadvantages:

  • Physically frail and reliant on their chemical arsenal.
  • Dangerous to their own side if their chemicals are mishandled.

Faction 2: The Sunless Order

The Sunless Order is a cult that thrives in the underground ruins of the old world...

Shadow Blades

Backstory: These assassins are trained in total darkness, relying on echolocation...

Advantages:

  • Exceptional stealth, able to ambush enemies undetected.
  • Fast and agile, with precise melee attacks.

Disadvantages:

  • Weak against bright light, which disorients them.
  • Limited ranged capabilities.

Burrowers

Backstory: Burrowers are engineers who pilot crude tunneling machines...

Advantages:

  • Can create new paths or fortifications during combat.
  • Heavily armored and hard to destroy.

Disadvantages:

  • Slow-moving and easy to outrun.
  • Vulnerable to attacks from above.

Void Speakers

Backstory: Void Speakers are the cult's mystics, claiming to channel the will of the underground...

Advantages:

  • Powerful crowd control abilities.
  • Can summon temporary allies to fight for them.

Disadvantages:

  • Weak in direct combat.
  • Their powers have a cooldown, leaving them vulnerable at times.

Faction 3: The Ash Marauders

The Ash Marauders are a nomadic tribe that roams the wasteland...

Dustriders

Backstory: Dustriders are expert raiders on customized wasteland vehicles...

Advantages:

  • Extremely fast and hard to hit.
  • Capable of attacking from multiple directions at once.

Disadvantages:

  • Their weapons have limited range and fuel.
  • Prone to self-destruction if their equipment is damaged.

Flamehowlers

Backstory: These pyromaniacs wield flamethrowers and incendiary bombs...

Advantages:

  • Devastating area-of-effect attacks.
  • Terrifies enemies, lowering their morale.

Disadvantages:

  • Their weapons have limited range and fuel.
  • Prone to self-destruction if their equipment is damaged.

Stormcallers

Backstory: Stormcallers are shamans who believe they can harness the wasteland's chaotic weather...

Advantages:

  • Can manipulate weather to create sandstorms or lightning strikes.
  • Capable of disorienting entire groups of enemies.

Disadvantages:

  • Unpredictable powers that can harm allies.
  • Vulnerable in close combat.
